=== MANAGEMENT COMMENTARY ===

CEO statement:
"Revenue growth of 15% reflects the full contribution of the Kellner Industrial
acquisition completed in February 2024. Organic revenue growth was 4.2%. Gross
margin compression of 150bps is attributable to elevated raw material costs and
integration costs within the Industrial segment. We expect margin recovery in H1
2025 as procurement synergies take effect. Net debt has increased to £298.8m
(prior year: £201.4m) following the acquisition. The board is targeting net
debt/EBITDA of below 2.0x by end of FY2025."
